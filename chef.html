CHEF

Mr Chef

The Heaven's Kitchen from horry, who competed on Top Chef Masters, is one of the best in the city, in terms of both food and ambiance. Located inside the historic Union Station in the city's growing downtown, it features a tapas-inspired menu with plenty of fresh seafood and local ingredients whenever possible. Despite the amazing food, many diners make the trip just for the atmosphere and decor which pays tribute to the history of union station with a classy and old-school style.

A master or executive chef is the ultimate leader of a kitchen and its staff. His/her role is to ensure exceptional customer service, and that requires menu and recipe creation, staff hiring, training, and management, food ordering, order expediting, and improving workflow processes in the back of house.

Becoming a chef isn't something that happens overnight, it is a journey that challenges you. Julia Child once said: “No one is born a great cook, one learns by doing”. Culinary arts require an undying devotion to what you do. It requires unconditional love for learning, cooking, and teaching others.
